Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29 June 2026

1. Introduction

Nimbly Hub (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates a Singapore-focused flexible work job platform. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, disclosed, and protected in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

2. Data We Collect

When you browse our website or submit a job application, you may provide:

  • Name and contact details, including mobile or WhatsApp number.
  • Work experience, skills, and job preferences.
  • Residency and work-eligibility information relevant to roles in Singapore.
  • Other information you choose to include in your application.

3. Purpose of Collection

We collect personal data to:

  • Process and forward job applications to the relevant employer or hiring partner for the role selected.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our job platform.
  • Respond to enquiries and communicate about platform-related matters.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. When you apply for a role, your application is shared with the hiring employer or partner company for that role. Where required by applicable law, we will obtain your consent before sharing your profile with a third party.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, or disposal.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to process applications, maintain platform operations, or meet legal requirements. You may request deletion subject to applicable law.

7. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you may request access to or correction of your personal data, or withdraw consent for certain uses, by contacting hello@nimblyhub.com.
