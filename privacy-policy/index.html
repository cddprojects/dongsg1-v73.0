Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5 June 2026

1. Introduction

Remote List (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

2. Data We Collect

By using the website and submission form, you may provide:

  • Name and contact information, including mobile or WhatsApp number.
  • Work experience and professional background.
  • Residency status, including Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident status.

3. Purpose of Collection

This data is collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To assess suitability for job opportunities and partner role types.
  • To communicate via WhatsApp or phone regarding job listings and applications.
  • To process job applications, with your consent.

4. Disclosure of Data

Your privacy is a priority. Personal data is not sold or rented to third parties. Your profile is only shared with partner companies after a personal conversation and your verbal or written consent.

5. Data Security

Appropriate administrative and technical measures are implemented to keep data confidential and secure at our CBD-based office.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to operate our job listing platform or respond to your enquiry, unless a longer period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal data, or withdraw consent at any time by contacting the team at hello@remote-list.com.
