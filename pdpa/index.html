Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Notice

Last Updated: 29 June 2026

Jobs Hutch is committed to protecting personal data in compliance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). This notice explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal data when you browse roles and apply for flexible work opportunities on our platform.

1. Collection of Personal Data

We collect personal data that you provide when submitting a job application or contacting us, including name, contact details, work history, job preferences, and eligibility information relevant to roles in Singapore.

2. Purpose of Use

Personal data is used to:

  • Process and forward job applications to the relevant employer or hiring partner for the role selected.
  • Operate and improve our job platform.
  • Respond to enquiries and communicate about platform-related matters.

3. Consent and Disclosure

By submitting an application, you consent to the collection and use of your personal data for the purposes above. Your application is shared with the hiring employer or partner company for the role you applied for. We will obtain additional consent where required before sharing your profile.

4. Data Security and Retention

We maintain reasonable safeguards to protect personal data and retain it only as long as necessary for platform operations or as required by law.

5. Access, Correction, and Withdrawal

You may request access to, correction of, or withdrawal of consent for your personal data by contacting our Data Protection representative at hello@jobshutch.com.
