Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Notice

At CivicPath Works, privacy is taken seriously. In compliance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA), this notice outlines how personal data is managed.

1. Collection of Personal Data

Name, WhatsApp number, work experience, and residency status are collected specifically to assess eligibility for career consultancy services and to advise on suitable partner role types.

2. Purpose of Use

Data is used solely for:

  • Assessing role suitability for career guidance purposes.
  • Contacting applicants via WhatsApp or phone for professional consultations.
  • Facilitating introductions to partner companies, only with your consent.

3. Consent & Disclosure

By submitting the form, you consent to being contacted for the purposes stated above. Personal data is never shared with partner companies without first having a personal conversation and obtaining your verbal or written consent.

4. Data Security & Retention

Data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorised consultants at our CBD office. Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide career consultancy services or respond to your enquiry.

5. Access & Withdrawal

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data at any time. For questions regarding PDPA practices, contact the Data Protection representative at hello@civicpathworks.sg.
